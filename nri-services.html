NRI SERVICES

Non -Resident Indians are an important part of India's success story. They represent India across the globe and are seen as ambassadors of the Indian nation. NRI’s have also contributed to the growth of Indian economy by repatriation of funds from abroad and strengthening the local economy. We help NRI’s to invest in Indian markets through various modes and instruments. With tieup with reputed banks, the entire process of investment for NRI’s is aimed to be a smooth and quick process.

NRI investors can invest in the following

  • Equity
  • Derivatives
  • Mutual Funds
  • Real Estate
  • Debt
  • Thematic Funds
  • ETF's
  • ULIP
