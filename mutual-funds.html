MUTUAL FUNDS

Mutual Funds are one of the most important modes of channelizing the savings of the nation into the economy. Mutual Funds work on the principle of pooling funds of various investors and investing them on a pooled basis to earn higher returns. MF’s in India are experiencing a very high interest from the burgeoning middle class and also helped by the growing digitalization of the economy. MF’s have become the preferred route of millions of investors to participate in the equity market of the county without actually having the skills to understand the valuation and other aspects of investing. MF’s being run by professional managers gives comfort to the investors to put their money in safe hands and well run funds.

Mutual Funds can invest in and be categorized as

  • Equity Funds
  • Debt Funds
  • Credit Funds
  • Hybrid Funds
  • Thematic Funds
  • ETF's
  • ULIP
