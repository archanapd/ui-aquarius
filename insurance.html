INSURANCE

Insurance is a tool to hedge against the unexpected events in ones life . Insurance mitigates and doesn’t compensate for the loss suffered. Such unexpected events ultimately affect the economic well being of the family and there is need to protect the same. Different types of Insurance products which help in such mitigation process are

  • Life Insurance- Premium paid on death or disability of the insured
  • Health Insurance- Claims paid on various type of Health emergencies and hospitalizations
  • Vehicle Insurance- Claims paid on any mishap and 3rd party claim. Helps in footing the bill for repairs
